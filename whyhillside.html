Why Hill Side Secondary School?

Hill Side Secondary School is a prominent educational institution located in Gotikhel, Lalitpur.
The school is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering an environment where students can develop holistically.

  • Comprehensive curriculum for classes up to 10.
  • State-of-the-art science and computer labs.
  • Regular assessments and progress tracking.
  • Focus on extracurricular activities like sports, arts, and crafts.
  • Dedicated and experienced teaching staff.
  • Library access with a wide range of books and study materials.
  • Interactive and engaging classroom environment.
  • Special focus on language development and communication skills.
  • Field trips and educational excursions.
  • Robust moral and ethical education.
